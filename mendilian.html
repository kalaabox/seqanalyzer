Mendelian Genetics Phenotypic Ratio Calculator

About This Tool

The Mendelian Genetics Phenotypic Ratio Calculator is an educational and research-friendly utility designed to predict phenotypic outcomes for both Monohybrid and Dihybrid crosses based on Mendelian inheritance laws. It provides expected phenotypic ratios and identifies the underlying genetic law applied in the cross, helping students and researchers visualize genotype-to-phenotype relationships.

This tool is ideal for teaching Mendelian principles, conducting quick classroom or lab simulations, and for industry-level training modules in genetics, plant breeding, biotechnology, and molecular biology fields.
