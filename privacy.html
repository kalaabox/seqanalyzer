Privacy Policy

Document Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines how SEQ Analyzer, a student-led bioinformatics project based in India, collects, uses, and protects user data. By accessing or using our tools and services, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. About Us

SEQ Analyzer is a student-driven educational platform dedicated to building accessible bioinformatics tools, learning resources, and data analysis projects for students, researchers, and educational institutions. As students from bioinformatics, pharmacy, and life sciences backgrounds, we aim to promote open knowledge and hands-on learning.

2. Data Collection and Usage

  1. Personal Information: We collect minimal personal information (such as name, email, and phone number) only through service request or feedback forms. This information is strictly used to contact users and deliver requested services.
  2. Usage Data: We may collect anonymized information such as tool usage statistics for academic reporting and platform improvement.
  3. No Sale or Third-Party Sharing: SEQ Analyzer does not sell, trade, or disclose any personal information to third-party organizations or advertisers.

3. Data Security

All collected data is stored securely using standard security practices. While we do not handle sensitive clinical data, we still prioritize the integrity and confidentiality of project and service records submitted through our website.

4. Educational and Non-Commercial Use

All tools, resources, and projects hosted on SEQ Analyzer are intended solely for educational, research, and academic demonstration purposes. Any decisions made based on our tools' outputs should be validated through certified clinical or industrial platforms before application.

5. Intellectual Property and Attribution

All digital tools, reports, and content available on SEQ Analyzer are created by our student team unless otherwise credited. Users may reference or use our tools for academic, teaching, or research purposes with appropriate attribution.

6. Future Amendments

As an evolving student initiative, we reserve the right to update this privacy policy in alignment with platform upgrades, security improvements, or academic project expansions. All users are encouraged to review this document periodically.
